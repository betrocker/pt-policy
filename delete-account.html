Delete Account

Last updated: 2025-12-21

This page explains how to request deletion of your PerfectTrade account and associated data.

Important: Deleting your account is permanent. After deletion, your journal entries, trade history, tags, and other data stored in the app may be removed and cannot be recovered.

What happens when you delete your account?

  • Your user account is deactivated and removed from our systems.
  • Journal data linked to your account (such as trades, notes, and tags) may be deleted or anonymized, depending on legal and technical requirements.
  • Your subscription with the App Store / Google Play is not automatically cancelled. You must cancel it in your store account settings.

How to cancel your subscription

Before or after requesting account deletion, we recommend cancelling any active subscription to avoid future charges:

  • Google Play: Open Google Play > tap your profile > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions > find “PerfectTrade” and cancel.
  • App Store (iOS): Open Settings > tap your name > Subscriptions > find “PerfectTrade” and cancel.

How to request account deletion

You can request account deletion in one of the following ways:

  1. From inside the app (if available):
    Go to Settings > Account > Delete account and follow the instructions.
  2. By email:
    Send an email to support@yourwebsite.com from the same email address used for your PerfectTrade account, with the subject line Delete Account Request.

Your email should include:

  • The email address linked to your account.
  • Your username or user ID (if available in the app).
  • A short sentence confirming that you want your PerfectTrade account and data to be deleted.

How long does it take?

After we receive your request, we will process the deletion as soon as reasonably possible, usually within 30 days, unless a longer period is required by law or technical constraints.
