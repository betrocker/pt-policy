Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-21

This Privacy Policy explains how PerfectTrade (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use the PerfectTrade mobile application (“App”). Using the App means you agree to this Policy.

1. Information You Enter

Depending on how you use the App, you may enter:

  • Trade entries, results, and journal notes.
  • Tags, checklists, rules, and screenshots related to your trades.
  • Other text you manually add inside the App.

This information is primarily used to help you track and analyze your own trading performance.

2. Account & Subscription Data

If you create an account or use Premium features, we may process information such as:

  • A user identifier (for example, from an authentication provider or database).
  • Your subscription status (active, expired, trial, etc.).
  • Basic billing metadata from the App Store / Google Play (we do not handle full payment details ourselves).

3. Information Collected Automatically

To keep the App reliable and improve it over time, we may collect:

  • Basic technical data (e.g. device model, OS version, app version).
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics.
  • Simple usage information (for example, which screens are used most), in aggregated or anonymized form where possible.

4. How We Use Information

We may use the information described above to:

  • Provide and operate the core journaling and analytics features.
  • Enable and manage Premium subscriptions.
  • Find and fix bugs, improve performance, and plan new features.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate with you if needed.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the App (for example, authentication, databases, analytics, crash reporting, or subscription/billing validation).
  • Authorities or third parties when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or allowed by law (for example, for legal, accounting, or security reasons).

7. Your Choices

  • You can stop using the App at any time. If you uninstall the App, local data on your device may be deleted.
  • If the App uses cloud sync or online backup, you may contact us to ask about deletion of stored data where applicable.
  • You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your App Store / Google Play account settings.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under the age required by applicable laws to give valid consent (for example, 13 or 16). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make important changes, we may notify you in the App or by other reasonable means. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated Policy.
